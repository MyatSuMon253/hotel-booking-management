Home Page

Hello, today I will demonstrate my Hotel Booking Management System.
This project is designed for Rangoon Heritage Hotel, a vintage-style hotel that wants to move from paper-based hotel operations to a digital web-based system.
The main purpose of this system is to reduce manual work, prevent booking errors, centralize hotel data, support online payment, and give administrators clear reporting for better decision-making.
The system has two main sides. For the customer side, they can browse rooms, make reservation for room and dinner, pay online, manage their profile, view invoices and write reviews.
The admin side, staff can manage rooms, customers, bookings, buffet dinners, promotions, membership tiers, reviews, and dashboard reports.

Room Browsing Screen

Now I will start from the customer side. This is the home page, where customers can browse available hotel rooms.
Each room card shows important information like room image, title, room type, price per night, capacity and is available.
This helps customers compare options quickly without contacting hotel staff manually.
On the right side, the system provides filtering options.
Customers can search by room name and filter by room type and availability.
This replaces the manual process of checking available rooms.
In this system, data is stored centrally and retrieved directly from the database.
All the list are paginated and can handle a large amount of data.

Room Detail

Screen action: Click one room, show `/rooms/:id`, image carousel, details, amenities, price, reviews. Script:

In room detail page, the customer can view a larger image carousel, the room description, type, price per night, and other facilities such as Wi-Fi, breakfast, and swimming pool access.
The detail page shows review and rating of the room, so customers can make informed decisions.
Customer cannot review without paying for this room.
On the right side, the booking form is shown and customer can make booking quickly.
I will demonstrate the room booking process. The booking form automatically fills the logged-in customer's name and email.
The customer can select start date and end date using a date range calendar.
This calendar uses disabled date from the backend, so reserved dates can be chosen.
This will prevent overlap bookings and reduces the risk of double booking.
The booking form calculates the number of rental days, rent per day, total rent, tax, discount, and estimated total amount.
There are two types of discount. If customer has a membership tier, the system applies the membership discount automatically.
But if the customer does not have a membership tier, they can enter a referral code from other member.
The system validates the referral code and applies 5% referral discount on rental fees.
The customer can also add an additional note, such as arrival time or special requests.
After confirming the information, click the Place Booking.

Checkout Page

After the booking is created, the customer is redirected to the payment page.
The system supports payment information and Stripe checkout integration.
Stripe handles the secure card payment with webhook technology.
After payment is completed, the customer can view the booking confirmation page.
This process is more reliable than manual payment handling because the booking and payment information are inter-connected in the database.

My Bookings Page

At the top, the system shows summary cards such as total bookings, unpaid bookings, and due amount.
Below that, the customer can manage bookings depending on the booking status.
They can continue payment, view confirmation, cancel booking, or downloading invoice.
This page also includes buffet dinner bookings, so the customer can manage both room reservations and dining reservations from one place.

Buffet Dinner Booking

Customers can view available buffet dinners from the list.
Each buffet dinner includes information such as title, cuisine category, description, image, start and end time, included dishes, remaining seats, price per guest, and facilities.
On the buffet detail page, the customer can choose the number of guests and create a dinner booking.
The system calculates the subtotal, tax, and total amount based on guest count and price per guest.
After booking, the customer can continue to checkout and payment confirmation page, the same as the room booking.
The system also tracks availabe seats, so staff and customers can see the remaining capacity.

Tourist Attractions Page

This page shows travel information related to local attractions.
For a heritage hotel, this feature improves the guest experience because most of the customers are foreigners visiting Myanmar.
Instead of only providing room booking, the system also supports trip planning and customer engagement.
It is demonstrating that the system is implemented based on customer journey, not only hotel operations.

Admin Dashboard

Now I will switch to the admin side.
The admin dashboard show a quick overview of hotel performance. including total revenues, total bookings, confirmed bookings and cancelled bookings.
The dashboard also includes visual charts of sales over time, booking trend, payment method distribution, and booking status distribution.
The date range calendar allows to change the reporting period more faster and accurate than normal ledger calculations.
This dashboard solves the main business problems of the old system that is difficult to generate timely and visual reports.

Room Management

Admin can create , update , delete rooms with full control and no longer need to maintain paper records.
When room data is updated here, customers can see the updated data immediately.

Booking Management

At the booking management page, Admin can monitor booking status, payment status, customer information and amount.
This gives the centralized operational view, which is very important for adminstration.

Customer Management

Admins can manage active status, membership tier, and referral points for customer.
The customer detail page show all the related bookings and loyalty sytem.

Promotion Management

Each promotion have a coupon code, discount type, discount value, start date and end date, maximum usage and used count.

Memebership Management

The membership module allows admins to define customer membershiptier levels and discount percentages.
This connects directly to the booking form, where membership discounts are applied automatically.
Together of promotions, referral codes, and membership tiers can improve customer retention and encourage repeat bookings.

Dinner Management

Admins can manage dishes, dinner time, capacity, price per guest and other facilities.
In the detail page, the included dishes are displayed with preview images.

Buffet Booking Management

In this page, admin can view buffet reservations and payment status.
Admin can also cancel the dinner bookings of customer in case of limited capacity.

Review Management

Admin can view customer feedback to improve hotel operations.
And also can delete the bad reviews to protects the hotel from inappropriate content.
The system supports a broader hotel operation that includes dining, customer feedback, and management control.

Profile Page

On the profile page, customer can update thier information and change password.
If the customers forgot their password, can request a reset email and create a new password using the reset token.

Closing

To conclude, this Hotel Booking Management System converts the hotel's manual paper-based workflow into a centralized digital platform.
Customers can browse rooms, register, make reservation for room and dinner, pay online, view invoices, manage bookings and submit reviews.
Administrators can manage rooms, bookings, customers, promotions, membership tiers, buffet dinners, reviews, and performance reports.
Overall, the system improves efficiency, reduces human error, increase data security through role-based access, and supports better business decisions through real-time reporting.
Thank you for watching my project demonstration.
